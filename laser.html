Laser Cutting and Woodshop

20 - 27 September 2018


This week, we used the laser engraver to create four different projects. We created clocks, press fit kits, a choice project, and water bottles. As with the printers last week, we used CorelDRAW to create our projects. The most important thing you will learn from my projects this week, if you had not already picked up on this from previous weeks, is that I am a nerd.
